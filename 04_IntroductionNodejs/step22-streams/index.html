Node.js Streams

The Secret to High-Performance Apps

1. The Definition

Streams are a way to handle data piece-by-piece. Instead of reading a whole file into RAM, we process it in small chunks.

"Think of a stream like a water faucet—you take sips as it flows rather than waiting for a 10-gallon bucket to fill up."

2. The Benefits

  • Memory Efficient: Process 10GB files on 1GB RAM machines.
  • Time Efficient: Data starts moving immediately without waiting for the full read.

3. Real-World Analogy

Think of YouTube vs. a Download. Traditional downloading requires the whole file first; Streaming lets you watch the first 10 seconds while the rest loads in the background.
